# Privacy Policy — DHRUVA

**Last updated: 18 July 2026**

DHRUVA ("the app") — *Dynamic Holistic Resilience & Unified Vigilance Architecture* —
is a real-time disaster and threat-intelligence monitoring app. This policy explains
what the app does and does not do with information. It is written to reflect the app's
actual behaviour.

## The short version

**DHRUVA does not require an account and does not collect, store, or share any personal
information about you.** There is no sign-up, no login, and no user profile.

## Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, precise or approximate location,
contacts, photos, or any other personal or device identifier. The app requests **no
device permissions** (no location, camera, microphone, contacts, or Bluetooth).

## Information stored on your device

To make the app usable, DHRUVA saves a few **preferences on your device only** (in the
app's local storage). This information stays on your device, is not transmitted to us,
and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its data. It includes:

- your display preferences (theme, content zoom);
- whether you have seen the introductory tour;
- your **watchlist** (regions and keywords you choose to follow) and notification
  preferences;
- a randomly-generated local session identifier used to keep the above preferences
  consistent on your device.

We never see this data.

## Network requests

DHRUVA displays live information by requesting data over the internet from public and
official sources, including (non-exhaustive): the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS), NASA
EONET, GDELT, abuse.ch / CISA, and India's SACHET / NDMA, routed through the app's hosted
backend. These requests are **read-only** and carry **no personal information** about you.

As with any app that connects to the internet, the servers that receive these requests
can see standard technical information such as your device's IP address and general
request metadata. This is inherent to how the internet works and is handled by those
third parties under their own privacy policies; it is not information that DHRUVA's
developer collects or retains.

All network communication uses encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

## Links to external sources

The app lets you open the original source article or report for an event. Opening such a
link takes you to a third-party website governed by that site's own privacy policy.

## Children's privacy

DHRUVA is intended for a general and professional audience and is not directed at
children under 13. Because the app collects no personal information, it does not knowingly
collect information from children.

## No advertising or tracking

DHRUVA contains no advertising, no analytics, no crash-reporting, and no third-party
tracking or marketing SDKs.

## Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change (for example, if optional accounts, analytics, or
push notifications are added), this policy and the app's store data-safety disclosures
will be updated, and the "Last updated" date above will change.
